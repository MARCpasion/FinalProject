HISTORY

It was only on July 2017 when Kamalig Garden Grill and Restaurant started its operation. The founder of the restaurant was inspired to it because of the demand of local customers, visitors from other provinces and tourists from other countries as well.

Laoag City is a very popular tourist destination in the north but there were only few restaurants existing in the city to cater the needs in the food industry. This made the idea of the founder to establish Kamalig Garden Grill and Restaurant and put it up right in the city proper but away from crowd and traffic jams.

"Kamalig" got its trade name from the Ilocano word "KAMARIN" or Kamalig in Filipino language, which means "a shed at the middle of a farm for farmers to rest at". It may also be called "rice granary". Kamalig Garden Grill and Restaurant serves the most popular Ilocano, Filipino, some Chinese and other international cuisines, grilled meat and seafoods as well. It also specializes in noodles where the company produces its own.

In just a very short period of time, Kamalig Garden Grill and Restaurant is now one of the most popular restaurants in the province where people celebrate, gather and get together. The restaurant has just opened the ACOUSTIC GARDEN GRILL where everyone can enjoy dining in the garden while listening to the acoustic band and guest singers can also jam with the band.

Kamalig Grill Restaurant was born in 2019 in the heart of Laoag City, inspired by the Filipino concept of kamalig, a traditional rice granary that symbolizes warmth, community, and the rich agricultural heritage of the Philippines.

Its design reflects a rustic charm, with nipa hut-style roofs and wooden interiors, creating a cozy atmosphere that feels like home.

The restaurant has grown to become a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike, celebrated not only for its mouthwatering Ilocano cuisine but also for its lively ambiance. Guests can enjoy live music performances, signature grilled dishes, and refreshing drinks while dining under the warm glow of Kamalig's open-air setting.

In a short span of time, Kamalig Grill has become synonymous with great food, cultural pride, and excellent hospitality, honoring its roots while continuously serving its growing community.
